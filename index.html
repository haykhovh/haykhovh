Let me introduce myself!

I am from Goris, Armenia.

Passionate about numbers from a young age, I have developed my entire professional career in line with my specialization. In addition to my studies, I have accumulated various experiences which have played an essential role in my personal and professional development. These experiences allowed me to confirm my interest in finances. The Master’s Program (Audit and Control) I chose is strongly linked to decision-making. As a future manager, I always try to relate my choices to predetermined principles which help me chart my professional and personal paths. I am passionate about audit, finance, programming and statistics.

My Work Experience.

January 2020 - February 2020

Grant Thornton France

Intern

I had the opportunity to do my School's final Internship in the Consulting department of Grant Thornton, Neuilly-sur-Seine. Being one of the best consulting firms in France, Grant Thornton gave me the opportunity to practice my theoretical skills in Audit and Accounting. Due to this experience, I gained knowledge in French Accounting Standards, IFRS, as well as French and International auditing standards.

May 2017 - January 2020

Aznavour Foundation

Researcher

Charles and Nicolas Aznavour founded the foundation in 2017 and I had the chance to be a part of its wonderful team from the very beginning. I started as an Event coordinating intern and then started to work as a Researcher and a Project coordinator.

July 2019 - November 2019

Himnark

Operations Manager

Himnark is a growing start-up in Armenia in Business Process automation. As head of operations, I was in charge of accounting and billing, Financial supervision and budgeting, as well as of Himnark's internal processes' control.

October 2018 - January 2019

CENTRAL BANK OF ARMENIA

Intern

I was lucky enough to do an internship at the Central Bank of Armenia, in Financial System Stability and Development Department. During this important experience I saw how to whole financial system works and how to maintain it as stable as possible. I worked mainly in Financial Stability Monitoring and Crises Management Division.
